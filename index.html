ALOHA

Congratulations on choosing our beautiful island to have your special day. Whether you are planning a sunset wedding on the beach or a ceremony under a Banyan tree, we offer a wide range of options from Tropical Flower Circles to Hanging Orchid Tree Garland. Everything Bloom Kauai does is specially designed and unique to your needs.

Occasions

Leis
​"Mohala i ka wai ka maka o ka pua" (Unfolded by the water are the faces of the flowers)

Leis are custom made with a variety of colors, fragrances, and textures. We offer orchids, plumeria, tuberose, and pikake. Maile leis are also available. Leis are a great gesture for the rehearsal dinner, birthday parties, or any special occasion.

Hakus and Flower Crowns
​"Fit for a Queen"

All Hakus and Flower Crowns are custom fitted. They can be as simple as ferns and orchids or as elaborate as you like with Mokara orchids, all local plants, or your favorite flowers.

Bouquets
“To Have and to Hold”

Choose your colors, style, size, and scent. Let us help you make the smartest choices for the season.

Ceremonies
"E Hoomau Maua Kealoha" (May our love last forever)

Let us make a ceremony never to be forgotten. You can choose from a Four Post Bamboo Arch covered with chiffon, flowers, and greenery, a Flower Circle made from Tropicals Flowers from Kauai, or an Isle of Orchids leading to the alter. Anything you can imagine, we can make happen. Please don't be limited to the website. If you have an idea or a picture, our job is to make it your reality.

Reception
"Dreams come true"

After your ceremony imagine Bloom Kauai providing a warm welcoming decor for your friends, family, and loved ones. Choose from candles, table runners table arrangements or, scattered orchids over Lawai ferns or palms Everything is custom to your design.

Don’t forget cake flowers, an arrangement for for the gift table, or a special arrangement for your sweetheart table!

Arrangements
"Me Ke Aloha pumehana" (with the warmth of my love)

Flower arrangements are an essential to every event. Bloom Kauai arrangements are made to order. We offer a variety of arrangements for all occasions including but not limited to entrance pieces, low table arrangements, vacation rental arrangements, sympathy arrangements, and birthday arrangements. Brighten up your surrounding with the gift of flowers.

Happy Delivery

We deliver flowers for all occasions or even no occasion. Receiving flowers bring out the best moods in all of us, even on the worst days in existence.

From The Garden

We grow our own Tropicals on Kauai. Flowers are cut within 24 hours of the event or delivery. So, you are always guarantied the freshest flowers on Island.
