What We Do

Bloom Kauai is dedicated to making unique one of a kind hand made flower designs. Bloom Kauai cuts all tropicals fresh from 40 acres of land located on the North Shore. Each flower is chosen distinctly for the arrangement or design within 24 hours of delivery.

Roses and Garden Flowers are imported from only the best flower farms from all over the world. Our designers are chosen because of their distinct artistic ability and love of the land and the flowers that it produces. Bloom Kauai takes pride island of Kauai and proud to call this home. We hope you enjoy the flowers and the island as much as we do.
