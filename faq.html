Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?
Bloom Kauai does not have a store front. We have a private farm and private studio. Flowers are delivered or arrangements can be made to pick up an order.
Do you deliver?
Yes, Bloom Kauai will deliver anywhere on island.
How do I pay?
Whether you place an order via email or over the phone, you pay by giving your credit card information over the phone for security and privacy reasons. You then will receive a receipt in your email.
When do I pay for my wedding order or event order?
Once you are have established an order, and you have approved the invoice 50% is due. Two weeks before the wedding or event payment is due in full.
Can I save the date before I decide on the order?
Yes, you can save the date by placing a $200 deposit. The $200 will be will later be subtracted from your invoice.
Can I change my order after I have placed my order?
Yes, you can change or add to your order up until 2 weeks before the wedding or event. Within 2 weeks of the wedding or event Bloom Kauai will do their best to accommodate you.
Does Bloom Kauai have flowers already made to be picked up immediately?
No, everything is made to order. It is possible to get something day-of, but it will take some time to be made.
What type of flowers do you have for immediate delivery?
Kauai Bloom has Tropicals, Roses, and some Mainland Garden Flowers to be delivered.
Do you ship to the mainland?
Yes, we can ship tropicals to the mainland.
Can we pick up our flowers to avoid delivery costs?
Yes, you can arrange a time to pick up your flowers in Kapaa.
How far in advance should I place an order?
The sooner the better. Orders can be made up to a year in advance.
Can I send a picture of what I would like?
Yes, if you find something you like, please email the picture.
How do I choose my flowers? Do I need to know the names?
No, if you know the colors or style you like, we will be happy to create something beautiful just for you.

Please feel free to call anytime to ask anymore questions or if you would like to chat. We are here to help you find exactly what you want and make your special day one not to be forgotten.
